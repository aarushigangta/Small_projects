About Founder

Alexa Wang is an American Photographer, based in Minneapolis. Wang makes "large-scale American projects" featuring the midwestern United States. New York Times art critic Hilarie M. Sheets wrote that he has made a "photographic career out of finding chemistry with strangers" and photographs "loners and dreamers".
